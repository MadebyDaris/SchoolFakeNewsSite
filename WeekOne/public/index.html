Fake UFO News

2020-05-03


The US Department of Defense has released three declassified videos of “unexplained aerial phenomena”. The Pentagon said it wanted to “clear up any misconceptions by the public on whether or not the footage that has been circulating was real”. The videos had already been leaked in 2007 and 2017. Two were published by the New York Times, while the third was leaked by an organisation co-founded by former Blink-182 singer Tom DeLonge.
